# I-765 Application for Employment Authorization Document (EAD) - USCIS Requirements

## Overview
Form I-765 is used to request employment authorization and an Employment Authorization Document (EAD).

## Eligibility Categories

### Common Categories:
- **(c)(3)** - F-1 student (OPT)
- **(c)(9)** - Adjustment of Status applicant (I-485 pending)
- **(c)(26)** - Spouse of E or L visa holder
- **(a)(5)** - Asylee
- **(a)(12)** - Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
- **(c)(35)** - H-4 spouse of H-1B visa holder
- **(c)(36)** - Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA)

## Required Documents

1. **Form I-765** - Completed and signed
2. **Two passport-style photos**
3. **Copy of I-94** (Arrival/Departure Record)
4. **Copy of passport** (biographical page)
5. **Copy of previous EAD** (if renewal)

### Category-Specific Documents:

#### For F-1 OPT (c)(3):
- Copy of I-20 with OPT recommendation from DSO
- Copy of F-1 visa and I-94

#### For Adjustment of Status (c)(9):
- Copy of I-485 receipt notice
- Evidence I-485 has been pending 180+ days (if applicable)

#### For Spouse of E/L visa (c)(26):
- Copy of principal's visa and I-94
- Copy of marriage certificate

#### For Asylee (a)(5):
- Copy of asylum approval notice
- Copy of I-94 showing asylee status

## Filing Fee
- **$410** (form fee)
- **$85** (biometrics fee, if required)
- Some categories exempt from fees

## Processing Time
- Generally 3-8 months
- Can request expedited processing in certain circumstances

## Validity Period
- Usually 1-2 years depending on category
- Can be renewed (file 180 days before expiration)

## Important Notes
- Must maintain valid immigration status
- Cannot work without approved EAD (except specific categories)
- Automatic 180-day extension available for certain renewal applications
- Different categories have different eligibility requirements
